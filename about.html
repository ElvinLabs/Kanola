Kanola Construction & Engineering



History

Inception of the enterprise was with a simple business idea of Managing Director, Mr. W M T P B Wijekoon. Launched company was registered in ICTAD as “Rajarata Construction” in 2002. Initiated with building construction & with the experience, he steered the company to expand the construction in Irrigation & Road Improvement fields.

Changing the momentum of business Mr. Wijekoon submerged himself in business arena in 2011; he re-established the company as “KANOLA”, & registered in ICTAD as Kanola Construction & Engineering. And he entered into the textile industry initiating Kanola Tex in Eppawala. It consists of all the types of textiles, gifts & electrical instruments in latest designs for affordable prices.

He has served and continues to serve in several apex bodies connected to the construction such as Asphalt, Interlock & Concrete plants. So he accompanied them in 2012 for strength up the construction field. It supplies raw materials as well as complete services with the incomparable skill workmanship.

In 2013 Mr. Wijekoon gathered new member of group, Kanola Auto & Heavy Machinery importing vehicles for sale. Luxury cabs & vans were the core business of this sector.

More recently he ventured into Kanola Tyres which imports & distributes tyres. It consists of tyres for all the types of vehicles with best quality & affordable price.

His knowledge and business acumen resulted in the success and continuation of the ventures and its expansion. Kanola will continue its progressive journey while bringing solutions that are more convenient for you.
